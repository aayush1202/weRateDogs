Analyzing and Visualizing Data: The final datasets were used to gather some insights. The following conclusions were generated:

  1. In which month were the most number of tweets made by the We Rate Dogs Twitter account?

The tweets made by the We Rate Dogs account peeked in the month of Dec 2015, as displayed in the graph below.

  1. Which dog breed is the most popular based on the number of tweets?

Based on the number of tweets, Golden Retrievers are the most popular dog breed.

  1. Which dog breed has the highest avg rating, amongst the top 15 most tweeted about dog breeds?

Amongst the top 15 most tweeted about dog breeds, Chow have the highest avg rating.

These conclusions are based on the data available with us, and may vary if the analysis is done on an extended dataset.
